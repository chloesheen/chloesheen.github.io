About Me


Hey there! I'm Chloe.
I'm in my final year of studying Computer Science and Mathematics at Bryn Mawr College.

This past month, I have been teaching web development at Hill Top Preparatory School for high school students with learning disabilities along with an introductory programming course in Python, my favorite language! On top of teaching, I have been doing research in speech synthesis using Festvox under the advisement of Jane Chandlee at Haverford College. In previous years, I worked as a software developer at an educational platform Lemma, a project manager at the Educational Technology department at Bryn Mawr, and as an instructor with TechGirlz.

As an avid writer and musician, I have also previously worked as a newspaper reporter, editorial columnist, and symphony orchestra violinist. Relevant portfolio is available upon request.
